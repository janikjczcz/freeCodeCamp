About Innovatex

Empowering Brands Since 2000

Innovatex began as a creative vision—bringing businesses closer to their audiences through custom promotional products. Founded by Ann, a dedicated entrepreneur with a passion for branding, Innovatex grew from humble beginnings into a leader in the promotional industry.

From its roots in gift basket creation to becoming a trusted partner in brand identity, Innovatex has evolved with its clients—ensuring marketing materials stand out, tell stories, and create lasting impressions.

At the end of the day, it’s not about finding a pen; it’s about finding the pen that increases your brand equity.
